Dolphinarium for young and old

Dolphin (Delphinus) is a genus of marine mammals from the dolphin family (Delphinidae). Translated from the ancient Greek δελφίς, δελφύς literally means "womb", "womb", i.e. "fish with a womb". Often, the word "dolphin" refers to all toothed representatives of the cetacean family (suborder dolphins, or "toothed whales"). In particular, this applies to three different genera of the dolphin family that are common in the marine waters of Ukraine: porpoises, bottlenose dolphins, and the dolphin itself.
